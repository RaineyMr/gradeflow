# GradeFlow Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 11, 2026

## What We Collect

**From Google OAuth:**
- Name, email, profile picture
- Used for account creation and authentication

**Educational Data:**
- Student grades, assignments, attendance
- Lesson plans and curriculum materials
- Teacher-parent communications
- School/district information

**Usage Data:**
- Login times and feature usage
- Platform performance analytics (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Data

- **Primary Purpose**: Provide educational services and platform functionality
- **FERPA Compliance**: Student education records are protected under FERPA
- **COPPA Compliance**: We comply with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act for K-12 students

## Data Protection

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Control**: Only authorized users can access student data
- **No Selling**: We never sell student or teacher data to third parties
- **Limited Sharing**: Data shared only with school districts as required for educational purposes

## Student Privacy (FERPA)

- GradeFlow operates as a "school official" under FERPA
- Parents have rights to access and amend student records
- Student education records are not shared without proper authorization
- Schools maintain primary control over student data

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)

- Users under 13 require parental consent for account creation
- Age-appropriate privacy protections are implemented
- Parents can review, delete, and refuse collection of their children's information
- No marketing directed to children under 13

## Your Rights

- **Access**: View your personal information and student records
- **Correction**: Request corrections to inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion (subject to legal requirements)
- **Export**: Download your data when leaving the platform

## Data Retention

- Student data retained according to state and federal requirements
- Accounts deleted upon user request (unless legally required to retain)
- Data automatically deleted when no longer needed for educational purposes

## Security

- Industry-standard security measures implemented
- Regular security audits and updates
- Staff trained on privacy and security protocols
- Incident response procedures in place
